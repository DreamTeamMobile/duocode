Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

DuoCode ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our real-time collaborative coding interview platform.

Information We Collect

DuoCode is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Session Data: Your display name (entered when joining a session) is stored only in your browser's local storage and shared with other session participants via peer-to-peer connection.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views and general usage patterns. This helps us improve the service.

Data Storage

DuoCode operates on a peer-to-peer architecture:

  • No Server Storage: Your code, diagrams, and messages are never stored on our servers. All data is synchronized directly between participants using WebRTC.
  • Local Storage: Session data is stored temporarily in your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB for the duration of your session.
  • Signaling Server: Our signaling server only facilitates the initial connection between participants and does not store any session content.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only data sharing occurs:

  • Between session participants via peer-to-peer connections (this is the core functionality of the service)
  • Anonymous analytics data with Google Analytics

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Theme Preference: We store your light/dark theme preference locally.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics may set cookies to track anonymous usage patterns.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All connections use HTTPS/WSS encryption
  • WebRTC connections are encrypted end-to-end
  • No sensitive data is stored on our servers

Your Rights

Since we don't store your personal data on our servers, there is no data to request, modify, or delete. You can clear your local browser data at any time to remove any locally stored session information.

Children's Privacy

DuoCode is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
